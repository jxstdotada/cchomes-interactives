The Myrtle Beach Equation (2026)

Myrtle Beach’s unique blend of strong tourism, retiree migration, and evolving STR regulations makes dual-purpose design a major 2026 advantage.

  • Stable STR occupancy forecasts (AirDNA)
  • Retiree migration driving long-term demand
  • SC rental disclosure law (§27-50) shaping resale transparency
  • Neighborhood standouts: Market Common, Belle Harbor, Carolina Forest

Universal Design Wins (Guests + Seniors)

Modern accessibility looks premium, photographs well, and extends your home’s long-term usability.

  • 36–42 inch hallways for mobility + luggage
  • Zero-step entry with wide landing
  • Lever handles for easier grip
  • Curbless shower + linear drain
  • Taller comfort-height toilets
  • Motion lighting for safety + convenience

High-ROI STR Features That Future-Proof

Guests love these features today. Retirees love them tomorrow. Resale wins either way.

  • Two primary suites (multigenerational flexibility)
  • Split-floor privacy layouts
  • Soundproofing between floors
  • Coastal-durable LVP flooring
  • First-floor essential living
  • Screened porch or low-maintenance patio

Smart Tech for STR + Aging

Convenience for guests. Safety and autonomy for aging residents. Protection for your property.

  • Smart locks with rotating codes
  • Video doorbells
  • Noise monitoring (non-recording)
  • App-controlled thermostat
  • Water-shutoff & leak sensors
